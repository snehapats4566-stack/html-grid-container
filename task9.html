Questions & Answers:

1. Why is 12-column grid popular?
Because 12 divides easily into 2, 3, 4 and 6, making layouts flexible.
2. How does spanning help layout?
Spanning allows sections to take proportional width for better alignment.
3. Where is this system used?
Used in Bootstrap, Tailwind, Material, dashboards, portfolios and UIs.
4. Can we create layouts without this system?
Yes, but 12-column systems make alignment faster and consistent.
5. What happens if column count changes?
Responsive breakpoints and spans need to adjust to the new structure.
Observation:
12-column grids balance flexibility and alignment for professional layouts.
